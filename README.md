## 🍯 The Secret Ingredient
Ever felt like you're working hard in the garden but your gold count isn't moving as fast?? 🐚 Cooking can be overwhelming with all the costs and star-rating luck! 

The **Heartopia Kitchen-Manager** is your new kitchen bestie. It’s a clever little brain that looks at your fridge and does all the "heavy thinking" (aka math!) to show you exactly which dishes will make you the most gold!. No more guessing, just more profit! 🍰💖

## 🍳 How to Cook Like a Pro
* **Check Your Pantry**: Type in how many ingredients you currently have. 🥚🥩
* **Calibrate Your Luck**: Enter your Star Probabilities! (Check the "Tips" box in the app for a quick trick to find yours!) ⭐
* **Magic Button**: Click **✦ OPTIMIZE EXPECTED PROFIT ✦** and watch the production plan appear! 🪄
* **Follow the Recipe**: Just follow the "Production Flow" at the bottom to start making those Gold! 💸

---

## 🍰 Chef's Special Extras
* ✨ **The 100-Salad Rule**: Want perfect accuracy? Cook 100 House Salads to find your exact star chances. It's the pro-gamer move! 🥗
* 🌒 **Midnight Snacking?**: Click the **🌓 Toggle Theme** if you're cooking in the dark to keep your eyes cozy.
* 🚫 **Level Locked?**: You can uncheck recipes you haven't learned yet so they don't show up in your plan!
